My Current Clubs and Organizations


NKU ROCKS

An NKU organization that promotes growth for African American incoming freshman.

WOMEN IN INFORMATICS

An NKU Club that is for all women in the college of informatics building that provides leadership and connections for future careers.

STUDENTS TO SCHOLARS

An NKU organization that helps sophomores transition into becoming a Junior. Students are provided with mentorship and network shops.

NATIONAL SOCIETY TO LEADERSHIP & SUCCESS

The NSLS offers tools to help individuals on their path to success. Individuals are offered connections and resources to help with their future careers

ALPHA LAMBDA DELTA HONORS SOCIETY

This honors society recognizes first year frreshman and their academic successes. Students are awarded a certificate at the end of the second semester.

DIVERSITY SCHOLARS IN INFORMATICS

This is an organization founded my myself and Dr. Anthony Tsetse that provides students of color in the NKU Informatics building with mentorship, resources, and networking skills.
